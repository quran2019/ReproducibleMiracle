7.1.1.2 Fact: Rewriting of Quran at verse level with sums

We then place those representation numbers where we see in the text as this is a rewriting test just as in the case of Ultimate Evidence, Evidence 7.1.1. Then we concatenate all the 6236 representation numbers and obtain the single representation number of the text of Quran considering only the numbered verses.
